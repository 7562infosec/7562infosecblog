Hello and welcome to my new blog. This is 7562 InfoSec Blog by James Sullivan (Sully). A few times a week I'll post on whatever's actually moving in the threat landscape — current incidents, notable vulnerabilities, and occasionally a look back at attacks that shaped how we defend today. No vendor marketing, no hype cycles. Just what matters to practitioners.

I am a cybersecurity practitioner and not just a researcher. I work in the industry and have for more than a decade. I've worked across all cyber domains at some point and extensively in vulnerability management and incident response. I take a defender’s view on cybersecurity, risk reduction and business enablement. Keeping up is hard especially with the new AI discovered vulnerabilities that is starting to come in. In legacy financial services, where a bulk of my experience is from, keeping up with patching was difficult before and only getting harder. I'll share my views and opinions and what I see "in the trenches".
